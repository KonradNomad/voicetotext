========================================
        VOICE TO TEXT
   How to Set It Up  -  Easy Steps
========================================


WHAT DOES THIS DO?

You talk, and the computer writes down your words.
That is all. It is easy.



SETTING IT UP  (you only do this one time)


STEP 1
Put these things in the same place (like the Desktop):

   - build.bat
   - voice_simple_win.py
   - voicetotext.ico

They came together in the same folder. Keep them together.


STEP 2
Find the file called   build.bat
Double-click it.  (Click it two times, fast.)

A black window will pop up. That is normal. Do not worry.


STEP 3
If the black window says you need "Python":

   - A website will open by itself.
   - Click the big yellow "Download" button.
   - Open the file it downloads.
   - VERY IMPORTANT:  On the first screen, look at the
     bottom. There is a little box that says
     "Add Python to PATH".
     CLICK that little box so it has a checkmark.
   - Then click "Install Now".
   - Wait for it to finish. Click Close.
   - Now double-click  build.bat  again.

If it did NOT ask for Python, good! Skip this step.


STEP 4
Wait a few minutes. The black window is working.
When it is done, it will say "All done!"

A new picture (a green microphone) appears on the Desktop.
It is called "Voice to Text".


STEP 5
Double-click the green microphone.
The app opens.


STEP 6  (one time only)
Look at the top-right corner. There is a tiny gear picture.
Click it.

A box opens. There is a line to type in a "key".
Paste the key that Konrad gives you.
Click the green "Save" button.

You are finished! Now you can use it.



HOW TO USE IT EVERY DAY

1. Double-click the green microphone on the Desktop.

2. Click the big GREEN button. It turns RED.

3. Talk. Say anything you want.

4. Click the big RED button when you are done.

5. A little spinning wheel shows it is working. Wait.

6. Then it says: "Click where the words should go..."
   You have 5 seconds. Click inside your email or
   document - wherever you want the words.

7. The words type themselves there. Like magic!

   (Your words also stay in the app window,
    so you can copy them again any time.)


THE OTHER BUTTONS

   COPY        - keeps your words so you can put them
                 in an email or somewhere else.

   READ ALOUD  - the computer reads your words back to you.

   START OVER  - clears the screen so you can begin again.



IF SOMETHING IS WRONG

Take a photo of the screen and send it to Konrad.
He will help.



TO REMOVE THE APP LATER

Open the app, click the tiny gear at the top-right,
and click the "Remove this app" button at the bottom.
It will clean everything up for you.
